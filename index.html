Bloom & Burn is a personal project built to combine two things I love — training hard and cooking meals that actually support those goals. Everything here is based on what's worked for me: real workouts, simple recipes, and understanding the numbers behind good nutrition. No gimmicks, no guilt — just feeling strong and fueled.
